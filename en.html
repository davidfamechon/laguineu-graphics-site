Who am I?

Philosophy and spirit of laguineu

Strategic design for brands that want to leave a mark.

Far from magic formulas, I believe in purpose-driven design. As a freelance graphic designer, web developer, and photographer in Barcelona, my job is to translate the essence of your project into a powerful visual experience. Whether it's shaping a new brand identity, designing an intuitive website, or capturing the perfect image, I blend creativity, technical precision, and functionality. My commitment is clear: to offer high-quality solutions, tailored to your needs, and always delivered on time. Let's work together to make your visual communication work for you.

Services

What can laguineu do?

Printed Advertising: Flyers, Posters, and Cards
Give your business visibility with high-impact physical media. From creative design to final printing, I handle it all: flyers, posters, business cards, and promotional brochures. I offer professional finishes and competitive prices to ensure your brand stands out everywhere.
Editorial Design: Catalogs, Books, and Magazines
I structure your content with rigor and elegance. I specialize in editorial layout for commercial catalogs, newsletters, corporate reports, and books. I fuse an impeccable aesthetic with a smooth reading experience to convey your information with the utmost clarity.
Visual Identity: Vinyls and Stickers
Transform your spaces into powerful communication channels. I design and produce decorative vinyls, window lettering, and custom stickers. A durable and aesthetic solution to reinforce your brand's presence on the street and capture the attention of new customers.
Web Development: Websites, Blogs, and Newsletters
Your digital window to the world. I create modern, fully responsive, and SEO-optimized websites. Whether it's a corporate site, a personal blog, or a professional newsletter, I help you build a solid online presence that converts visitors into customers.
Photography, Audiovisuals, and Content Management

A professional image is worth a thousand words. I offer advertising photography and audiovisual content creation services to highlight your products or services. I master digital retouching techniques to guarantee high-end results.

Additionally, through my network of collaborators, I can manage professional translations into several European languages, ensuring maximum technical precision for your international projects.
